What is curiosity?

By definition, curiosity can be briefly expressed as “A desire to know or learn”. When you are curious about a subject, it means that you want to know more about it. To make it very clear before going into further analysis, when I mention the term curious, what actually I imply is the curiosity for useful knowledge. It’s when you ask questions like: “How do I do this task more effectively?", “Why do I need to study maths?", .. not the type of curiosity about others' personal stuffs, such as “Why haven’t you married yet?" or “What do you do?". The later is either good or bad, depending on the questioners' intention, but the former is always good.
